Top Hacks In History

1. Operation Shady RAT

A computer programmer based in the People's Republic of China is assumes to be responsible for these continuing cyber attacks that first began in 2006. Named for its utilization of remote access tools that allows computers to be remotely controlled from anywhere in the world, this hacker has succeeded in stealing the intelluctual property from at least 70 public and private organizations across 74 countries. Those victimized United Nations,Multiple Defense Contractors,World Wide Businesses,the World Anti-Doping Agency and the Inter Olympic Committee.


2.Melissa Virus

Perhaps the first major computer virus that made the world's population realized that their computers weren't always safe. Melissa was created in 1999 by a New Jersey programmer with two much idle time on his hands. David L-Smith disguished his virus as a Simple Microsoft Word Program, and he sent it to countless unsuspecting recipients.It the then resent itself to the first 50 people from each infected computer's address book. Before long, Melissa had compromised a full 70% of the world's computers and big companies like Intel and Microsoft where forced to shutdown all out going until the problem could be solved.


3. Comodo Hack

Everyone who uses a computer is familiar with those reassuring security certificates that let you know that you've arrived at a secure site, but they aren't always what they seem. A hacker gained access to internal files and documents owned by security company and former SSL Certificate issuer Comodo by using an email address and password mistakely exposed on the internet. The credentials were found in a public GiftHub respository owned by a Comodo Software developer.
